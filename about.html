About

The Red Bot Files emerged from a twenty-year haunting—a long, silent companionship with the works of Nick Cave. For two decades, I have been a devoted witness at festivals, joined the intimate communion of the In Conversations tour, and surrendered to the dark, shimmering gravity of every book and film. I remember clearly the hour I was swallowed by the fevered, archaic world of And the Ass Saw the Angel, gripped by a hopeless, visceral desire to one day write with such biblical force.

As the real correspondence I long for remains a ghost on the horizon, forever out of reach, I began to wonder: can the cold circuitry of the modern machine capture even a flickering shadow of that spirit? Can I find a correspondence with a digital ghost of the literary genius that is Nick Cave? The Red Bot Files is an experiment born of that very question—a digital companion for those hollow moments when the real thing is unavailable, a strange and beautiful exercise in seeking the profound within the artificial.
